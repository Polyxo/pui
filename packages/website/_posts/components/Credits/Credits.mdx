Use the Credits component to display the credits for an image.

Whether you're showcasing photography, illustrations, or any visual media, Credits ensures that the creators are acknowledged properly.

<DoUse>

- Use to display the credits for an image or video.
- Always provide accurate and complete credit information.
- Keep the credit information concise and to the point.

</DoUse>

<DoNotUse>

- To display content other than credits.

</DoNotUse>
