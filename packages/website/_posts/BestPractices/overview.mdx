This gives a set of guidelines, principles, and standards that are established to
ensure consistency, efficiency, and effectiveness in the creation, maintenance, and implementation of
design components and patterns. These practices aim to streamline the design and development processes,
enhance collaboration among team members, and contribute to a cohesive and user-friendly experience across
products and platforms.
